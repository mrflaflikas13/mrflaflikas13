International Competitions

Are you wondering which are the international competitions in soccer? Here is a list of the most featured and popular ones ( click here to find out more of them):

  • FIFA World Cup (Every 4 Years, Global)

  • FIFA Women's World Cup (Every 4 Years, Global)

  • FIFA Confederations Cup (Every 4 Years)

  • Football At The Olympics (Sub-23 Competition)

  • UEFA EURO (European Teams Only)

  • Copa America (Teams From America)

  • Asian Cup (Teams From Asian Continent Only)

  • African Cup of Nations (African Teams Compete Against Each Other)

  • UEFA Champions League (4 Teams From The Top 8 European Leagues)

  • UEFA Europa League (Teams That Get Knocked Out of Champions League Participate!)

  • FIFA Club World Cup (Champions of Each Confederation Go Head-to-Head Against Each Other)

  • UEFA Nations League (European Nations Only)
